BizLink

BizLink is an innovative e-commerce platform built for small business owners in Sri Lanka. Instead of struggling to reach customers individually, entrepreneurs can easily create their own virtual store, list products, and showcase their brand. BizLink empowers local businesses to grow by providing a simple, modern, and affordable way to sell online.

Responsive Design, SEO

The BizLink website is fully responsive, designed to deliver a smooth shopping experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. From browsing items to managing your store, every step is optimized for speed, simplicity, and convenience—helping small businesses reach more customers effortlessly.

I designed and developed the complete frontend of BizLink, focusing on a clean and modern UI, mobile-friendly layouts, and user-friendly features. This ensures that both shop owners and customers enjoy a professional, engaging, and reliable online shopping experience.
